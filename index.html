I am a software engineer from Los Andes University, Bogotá. I am currently working at Eindhoven University of Technology as a PhD student in the Software Engineering and Technology (SET) group. My interests focus on research and teaching. The main topics I consider in my research are related to software analysis and software evolution. All publications are available in Google Scholar.
